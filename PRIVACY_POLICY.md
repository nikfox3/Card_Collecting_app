## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Username, email address, password (hashed), full name, profile image
- **Collection Data**: Cards in your collection, quantities, conditions, purchase prices, purchase dates, notes
- **Deck and Binder Data**: Custom decks, binders, and associated card information
- **Pricing Alerts**: Card alerts, target prices, and notification preferences
- **Profile Settings**: Privacy preferences, notification settings, theme preferences

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: Search queries, card views, feature usage, interaction patterns
- **Device Information**: Browser type, device type, operating system
- **Session Data**: Session tokens, authentication status

### 2.3 Third-Party Authentication
- **Google OAuth**: If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your Google account email and profile information (name, profile picture) as provided by Google.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and maintain our service
- Process your transactions and manage your account
- Send you notifications about price alerts and account activity
- Improve our services and user experience
- Analyze usage patterns and trends
- Ensure security and prevent fraud
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage**: Some data (like collection preferences) may be stored locally in your browser
- **Database Storage**: Your account data, collections, decks, and binders are stored securely in our database
- **Security Measures**: We use industry-standard security practices including password hashing, session tokens, and secure database connections
- **Data Retention**: We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

- **Public Profiles**: If you enable "Public Profile" or "Public Collection" settings, your profile and collection information may be visible to other users
- **Service Providers**: We may share data with third-party service providers who assist in operating our service (e.g., hosting, analytics)
- **Legal Requirements**: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety

## 6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View and download your collection data (available in Settings)
- **Update**: Modify your account information and privacy settings
- **Delete**: Request deletion of your account and associated data
- **Opt-Out**: Disable notifications and analytics tracking
- **Export**: Download your collection data as CSV

## 7. Cookies and Tracking

- We use session tokens to maintain your login status
- We may use analytics to understand usage patterns (you can opt-out)
- We do not use third-party advertising cookies
